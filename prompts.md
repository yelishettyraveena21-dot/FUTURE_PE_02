# Prompt Logic

## Goal

Generate high-converting UGC advertisements for a Herbal Hair Growth Oil.

## Prompt Strategy

1. Defined the AI role as a professional UGC creator.

2. Defined the target audience:
   Women experiencing hair fall.

3. Defined the platform:
   Instagram Reels, Facebook, and YouTube Shorts.

4. Defined the tone:
   Authentic, relatable, emotional, and conversion-focused.

5. Defined the structure:
   Hook → Problem → Personal Experience → Solution → Results → CTA

## Reason

Structured prompts help generate more targeted, engaging, and marketing-focused content. By clearly defining the audience, tone, platform, and format, the AI produces content that feels natural and suitable for UGC advertising.
